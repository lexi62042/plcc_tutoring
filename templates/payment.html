Frequently Asked Questions

How do tutoring sessions take place?

All tutoring sessions are conducted online via Zoom or Google Meet. This allows for flexible scheduling and enables us to serve students from around the world. Screen sharing, digital whiteboards, and other collaborative tools ensure an interactive learning experience.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no penalties for changing plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied after your first tutoring session, we'll refund your payment in full. For ongoing subscriptions, you can cancel at any time, but we do not provide partial refunds for unused sessions.

How do I schedule my tutoring sessions?

After signing up, you'll receive access to our online scheduling system where you can book sessions based on Pia's availability. The Premium Plan members receive priority access to scheduling.
