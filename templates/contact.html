Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line directly.

Do you offer in-person tutoring?

Currently, all our tutoring sessions are conducted online. This allows us to provide our services to students worldwide while maintaining flexibility and accessibility.

I need immediate help before my exam. Can you accommodate?

Yes! We understand that exam periods can be stressful. Our Exam Booster package is designed specifically for last-minute preparation. Contact us with your specific needs, and we'll do our best to schedule urgent sessions.

Can I speak directly with Pia before signing up?

Absolutely! We offer a complimentary 15-minute consultation with Pia for all potential students. Use the contact form and select "Tutoring Inquiry" to request your consultation.
